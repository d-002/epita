Extensions install guide for Chromium

  • Download the extension as a zip file, then unzip it somewhere persistent (like your AFS on the PIE).
  • Open your browser and go to chrome://extensions (or the name of your browser, like opera://extensions.
  • Toggle Developer mode on.
  • Click on Load unpacked.
  • Select the directory you just unzipped.

Notice: when the extension receives an update, you should replace the extension directory by the new one.

Until further notice, my extensions do not auto-update, to allow you to have greater control over your data.
